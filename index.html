Aurora Zabot

I'm Aurora Zabot, a recently graduated student in the ICT field, with a study path mainly focused on multimedia, computer vision and machine/deep learning.
I decided to follow this kind of path because of my passion for both technology and art, trying to find a meeting point between these two worlds.

I was born in Feltre, Italy, in 1996. I got my B.Sc in Information Engineering in 2015 and my M.Sc in ICT for Internet and Multimedia both from the University of Padua.
My main focus and interests in work and research are related to Multimedia Processing, Perceptual Analysis and AR/VR.
